Race History

Philosophy​

I know a lot of people who will train hard for three or four months for a marathon, run a strong race, and then do nothing for a few months. This always seemed wasteful to me. If you spend the time building up the fitness, I'd rather continue to use it. That's why I race. A lot. I also really love the trail running community. The community is why I keep coming back.
